Easy and Delicous Cesar salad!

Crisp Perfection: Caesar Salad Unveiled

  • Toss and Dress: Combine crisp romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing, tossing until each leaf is coated.

  • Top and Enjoy: Sprinkle with grated Parmesan and add croutons for a crunchy finish. Serve immediately for a classic Caesar salad delight.


Ingrediants

  • Romaine Lettuce: Crisp and hearty, torn into bite-sized pieces
  • Garlic: Minced or crushed garlic can be added to enhance the overall flavor.
  • Parmesan Cheese: Grated or shaved for a salty and nutty flavor.
  • Salt and Pepper: For seasoning to taste.
  • Croutons: Cubes of toasted or baked bread for added crunch

Quick Guide:

  1. Prepare Dressing: In a bowl, whisk together minced garlic, Dijon mustard, anchovies, egg yolks, Parmesan cheese, lemon juice, and olive oil to create the Caesar dressing.
  2. Tear Romaine Lettuce: Wash and tear the Romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Place it in a large salad bowl.
  3. Coat with Dressing: Drizzle the prepared Caesar dressing over the lettuce, ensuring even coating. Toss the salad gently to incorporate the dressing.
  4. Add Parmesan: Sprinkle grated or shaved Parmesan cheese over the salad, distributing it evenly
  5. Add Croutons: Introduce croutons to the salad for a satisfying crunch. Adjust the quantity based on preference.
  6. Season to Taste: Add salt and pepper to taste. Toss the salad again to ensure all ingredients are well combined.
  7. Optional Additions: Include optional additions like grilled chicken, bacon, cherry tomatoes, or hard-boiled eggs for extra flavor and protein.
  8. Serve Fresh: Plate the Caesar salad and serve immediately, ensuring the crispness of the lettuce and the flavors of the dressing are enjoyed at their best.
