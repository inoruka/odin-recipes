Arabiata_Pasta

Cooked Pasta with Arabiata souce, Green Oviles and Tuna.Toped with a Sprinkl of Citrus.

  • First Step: Cook the Pasta aldente with enough salt and Oil and Then put it on low heat on a Pan with a Big spoon of the Pasta water.

  • In the Second Part: you gotta add the Arabiata, a couple of olives and some una and Mix it good while it keeps Boiling and vaporing the Pasta Water off, once it looks quite good test taste it and add if needet some salt and pepper.


Ingrediants

  • Pasta
  • Arabiata
  • Green Olives
  • Salt/Peeper
  • Tuna
  • citrus

Quick Guide:

  1. Cook The Pasta aldente
  2. Sieve the noodles and lay them in a pan on Medium heat
  3. Now add the Arabiate,the Tuna and The olives
  4. Let it cook!
  5. Take it out, taste test with some Salt and peper and add a squirt of Lemon juice
